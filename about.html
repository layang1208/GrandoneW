About

Tucked away in a smallv food court off Don Mills in North York,

Grandone Chicken aims to make big waves in
Toronto’s fried chicken scene. Grandone Chicken was opened up in December, 2020.

We are committed to bring high-quality, made-to-order fried chicken and seafood to your table, and all at a reasonable price.

Sweetening the deal even further, Grandone offers
the entire menu for 50% off until May 4th. This promo is available in-store and across all major delivery apps.

If you like wings, tenders, nuggets or sandwiches,
we’ve got everything to satisfy that crunchy fried
chicken craving. Also, try our seafood options like crispy fish, calamari and shrimp sandwiches.
